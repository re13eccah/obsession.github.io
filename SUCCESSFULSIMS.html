THE SECRETS TO SUCCESSFUL SIMS

USE OTHER SIMS FOR BUSINESS

The best way to make money is to TAKE money. It is NOT morally wrong to marry a rich sim and then remove the pool ladder, if you catch my drift.

SIMOLEONS > EVERYTHING

Sure, your sims may have a "needs" panel, but all I'm asking is do you even really care about your sims being successful if they're not in the red? Simoleons should take precedence in any reasonable sim's life.

ENIMIES MAKE ENVY

Enemies are the best propellant for success. Seeing a sim your sim despises do better than you will be all the fuel one needs to become the ruling leader of the Sim Empire.
